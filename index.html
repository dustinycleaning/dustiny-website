About Us

Dustiny Cleaning Services was founded on April 1, 2025, by Erick Ombui with a vision to provide top-tier cleaning solutions for homes and businesses. Motivated by a passion for cleanliness and customer satisfaction, Erick saw a gap in professional, reliable cleaning services. Through dedication and innovation, Dustiny Cleaning Services has grown to become a trusted name in the industry. Our goal is to create cleaner, healthier environments for our clients while delivering excellent customer service and efficiency.
